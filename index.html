About MassFinder

MassFinder helps Catholics across Western Massachusetts find Mass times, Confession, Adoration, and other services at parishes in the Diocese of Springfield.

All service times are sourced from parish bulletins and verified periodically. If you notice an error, please use the "Submit a Correction" link below.
